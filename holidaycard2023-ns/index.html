About the Artist

Jihye Shin is a Chicago-based visual artist. Her work captures the essence of dynamic urban scenes—the nature and the people who surround her. During her artistic process, she strives to create harmonious fusion between the traditional style from her heritage and western environments. Her recent work aims to portray the colorful and cheerful mood of the rides and buildings at Navy Pier. She received her BFA from Hong-ik University in South Korea in Oriental Painting and currently resides in Hyde Park.

See more of Jihye Shin's work at www.shinjihye.com.

Hyde Park Art Center

Hyde Park Art Center is a hub for contemporary arts in Chicago, serving as a gathering and production space for artists and the broader community to cultivate ideas, impact social change, and connect with new networks. The Art Center functions as an amplifier for today and tomorrow’s creative voices, providing the space to cultivate and create new work and connections.
